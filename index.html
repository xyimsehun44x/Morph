Process

How The Morph Works

Three AI-powered phases turn your photos and biometrics into a visual transformation roadmap.

01

Input & Biometrics

Upload a clear front-facing photo plus your target physique. Enter age, height, weight, body fat estimate, and training history. The AI needs your blueprint before it can build your roadmap.

02

The Morph (Generative AI)

Using Stable Diffusion with ControlNet and IP-Adapter, the AI preserves your facial identity and bone structure while projecting realistic muscle development — scaled to your actual skeletal frame, not the model's.

03

Your Plan Architect

Computer vision analyzes the morph for muscle imbalances. GPT-4 then builds a lagging-part specialization workout routine and dynamic hypertrophy nutrition plan — both tied directly to the visual outcome.

Ethics & Safety

Built-In Guardrails

Fitness AI has real psychological stakes. These aren't disclaimers — they're core features that make the app worth trusting.

Genetic Reality Score

Every generated morph includes a "Genetic Probability Score." The AI explains which muscle shapes are unique to the reference physique's insertions and cannot be replicated through training alone — preventing impossible chasing.

Enabled by default

PED Reality Check

If a "Natural Athlete" inputs an extreme chemically enhanced physique as their target, the AI automatically generates a tested/natural equivalent instead — with a clear explanation of why the original is not achievable without PEDs.

Automatic detection

Recursive Progress Truth

Every 4-week scan re-anchors the projection to your real body's response. If you're growing slower than predicted, the morph timeline adjusts. The app never lets you chase a version of you that your actual body has already diverged from.
